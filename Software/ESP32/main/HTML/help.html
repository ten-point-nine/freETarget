The web browser application in the FreeETarget has two modes of operation.

1 – Manual – The target outputs the accumulated shots then stops.

      You must press refresh on your browser to get the next shots.

2 – Automatic – The target outputs the accumulated shots and any new ones that appear.

      You must manually terminate the session.
